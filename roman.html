About Roman Reigns

Roman Reigns, born Leati Joseph Anoa'i, is a professional wrestler, actor, and former professional gridiron football player. He is signed to WWE, where he performs on the SmackDown brand and is the reigning Undisputed WWE Universal Champion.

Known as “The Tribal Chief”, Roman Reigns is the leader of The Bloodline and one of the most dominant figures in WWE history. With multiple championship reigns, he has headlined several WrestleMania events and brought a powerful presence to the ring with his charisma and athleticism.

Roman has Samoan heritage and belongs to the legendary Anoa'i wrestling family, making him a natural-born superstar. Outside the ring, he’s also a cancer survivor and an advocate for leukemia awareness.
